# Rural Retreat: Sustainable Cabin Masterplan

Nestled in the heart of nature, this cabin masterplan represents a perfect harmony between modern architecture and environmental consciousness. The project encompasses multiple cabin units strategically placed to maximize views while minimizing environmental impact.

## Design Philosophy

The design emphasizes:
- Sustainable materials
- Minimal environmental footprint
- Natural light optimization
- Integration with landscape

## Technical Details

- Total Area: 5,000 sqm
- Number of Cabins: 12
- Sustainable Features:
  - Solar panels
  - Rainwater harvesting
  - Natural ventilation
  - Local materials

## Impact

This project sets a new standard for sustainable rural development, proving that luxury and environmental responsibility can coexist beautifully.
